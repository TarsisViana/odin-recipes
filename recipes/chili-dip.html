Chili Dip

Description

It's great for parties and football get-togethers. With just three ingredients, it's very easy to prepare. Serve it hot with your favorite corn chips.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese: softened.
  • 1 (15 ounce) can chili without beans.
  • 1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers: drained.

Steps:

  1. In a medium, microwave safe bowl, mix cream cheese, chili without beans and diced tomatoes with green chile peppers.
  2. Microwave cream cheese mixture on high 1 minute. Remove from microwave, stir and repeat until the mixture is hot and thoroughly blended.
